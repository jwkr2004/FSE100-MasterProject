How To Play

Your goal is to match cards by number and suit in as few clicks as possible. All of the cards will be covered. When a card is clicked, it is revealed. When a second card is picked, it is also revealed. If they are a match, the cards disapear. If they don't match, the cards are flipped back around. You can select the number of cards to use below.
