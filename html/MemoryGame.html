How To Play

Choose your desired number of cards to match (The more cards, the more difficult!). Click on a card to flip it over. Match the pairs of cards by flipping over two identical cards (7 of hearts matches with 7 of hearts, Queen of diamonds with queen of diamonds, etc.). You must rely on memory and concentration to match the pairs in the least amount of time. Good luck!
